Styling flow

Finish the face and hands before adding more.

  • 1Choose the nail finish first: glossy nude, soft shine, clean color, or a small accent.
  • 2Keep lashes soft enough to open the eyes without overpowering the rest of the beauty look.
  • 3Let nails and lashes carry the final polish, then keep jewelry and makeup clean around them.
Glossy nails soften the hands. Lifted lashes brighten the face. Together, they make everyday beauty look more intentional.

Beauty direction

Keep the finish polished, not heavy.

Soft nails and delicate lashes work best when the finish looks clean from close up. Neutral tones, glossy texture, and lifted eyes can make simple beauty feel more refined.

For everyday styling, choose smaller details with a softer shape. For a dressier look, lean into extra gloss, a more defined lash line, or a brighter nail accent.
