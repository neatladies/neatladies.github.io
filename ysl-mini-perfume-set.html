A refined fragrance wardrobe in miniature form.

This YSL collection combines four beloved fragrances in elegant travel sizes that feel polished, feminine, and gift-ready. The set offers a beautiful balance between warm sensual notes, clean florals, soft musks, and modern Paris-inspired sweetness, making it easy to switch between moods, occasions, and seasons.

BLACK OPIUM

Warm coffee and vanilla with a bold evening character.

Black Opium brings together rich coffee notes, creamy vanilla, and soft white florals in a fragrance that feels modern, deep, and addictive. It adds a darker contrast to the set while still keeping the elegant YSL softness throughout the collection.

LIBRE EDT

A lighter floral blend designed for daytime elegance.

Libre EDT feels fresh, airy, and softly feminine with lavender, orange blossom, and delicate tea-inspired freshness. It works beautifully for everyday wear while maintaining the sophisticated YSL signature style.

LIBRE EDP

Floral warmth with a richer and more sensual finish.

Compared to the EDT version, Libre EDP introduces deeper musk and cedar warmth while keeping the recognizable floral lavender identity. The result feels smoother, richer, and more refined for evenings and special occasions.

MON PARIS

Bright berries and floral sweetness inspired by Paris romance.

Mon Paris introduces fruity brightness and soft floral sweetness into the collection through raspberry, strawberry, citrus, and white floral notes. It feels playful yet polished, balancing the darker richness of Black Opium perfectly.
