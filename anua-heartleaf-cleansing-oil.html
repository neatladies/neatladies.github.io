Why it belongs in the routine

A better evening cleanse starts before the water-based cleanser.

A cleansing oil is the step that helps break down the day first: sunscreen, base makeup, sebum, and surface buildup. The goal is not to make the routine longer — it is to make the rest of the routine feel cleaner and more effective.

How to use it

Keep the routine simple, clean, and consistent.

1

Start dry

Massage the cleansing oil onto dry skin so it can work through sunscreen, makeup, and daily buildup before water is added.

2

Emulsify

Add a little water and continue massaging until the oil turns milky, then rinse thoroughly and avoid rushing this step.

3

Follow with cleanser

Use a gentle water-based cleanser after rinsing, then continue with the rest of your evening skincare routine.

What to expect

Expect a first-cleanse step that helps your skin feel more prepared for the rest of the routine. It is especially useful if you wear SPF daily or prefer a cleaner base before applying treatment products at night.

What not to expect

Do not treat any cleansing oil as a miracle product or a replacement for a complete routine. Patch test when needed, avoid the eye area if you are sensitive, and follow the product label instructions.
