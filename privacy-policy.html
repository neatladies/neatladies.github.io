Neatladies is an editorial shopping guide. Visitors can browse the site without creating an account or submitting personal information. Basic technical and analytics data may be collected to understand site performance and improve the browsing experience.

Information we may collect

Neatladies may collect limited non-sensitive information through analytics tools, tracking tags, browser data, and normal website logs.

Neatladies uses services such as Google Analytics and Pinterest Tag to measure visits, page interactions, and outbound clicks. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies to provide aggregated reporting.

Neatladies participates in affiliate programs. When visitors click affiliate links, third-party retailers or affiliate platforms may use cookies or tracking technologies to attribute qualifying purchases.

Product links may lead to external websites such as Amazon or other retailers. Neatladies is not responsible for the privacy practices, policies, or content of third-party websites. Visitors should review the privacy policy of any external site they choose to visit.

Cookies

Cookies may be used by analytics, advertising, affiliate, or third-party services. Visitors can control or disable cookies through their browser settings.
